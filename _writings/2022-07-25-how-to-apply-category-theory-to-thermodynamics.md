This post discusses the paper *Compositional Thermostatics* by John Baez, Owen Lynch, and Joe Moeller. The broader [series of posts on Dr. Baez's blog](https://golem.ph.utexas.edu/category/2022/06/compositional_thermostatics_1.html) offers a fuller primer on the paper's background, while this piece takes a more selective path through the framework.

Like those posts, this essay explains some parts of the framework in an introductory way. It also emphasizes a few details that seemed especially interesting to me and closes by treating a particular quantum system using ideas from the paper.
