How to Apply Category Theory to Thermodynamics

July 25, 2022 Notes

This post discusses the paper Compositional Thermostatics by John Baez, Owen Lynch, and Joe Moeller. The broader series of posts on Dr. Baez's blog offers a fuller primer on the paper's background, while this piece takes a more selective path through the framework.

Like those posts, this essay explains some parts of the framework in an introductory way. It also emphasizes a few details that seemed especially interesting to me and closes by treating a particular quantum system using ideas from the paper.
